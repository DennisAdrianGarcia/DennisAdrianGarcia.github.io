This webpage is about how to properly take care of your fur babies.

Dos:

Feed your pet/s properly

Don't overfeed your pet, but still make sure that they are getting the right amount of food.
Most dogs needs about 25 to 30 calories per day.

Dont's:

Don't use force as a deterrent

If your dog did something wrong, do not use violence as a deterrent,
you might harm your dog or even yourself.

Here are some foods that you shouldn't feed to your dog.

-Garlics, Onions, And Chives

-Chocolate

-Grapes and Raisins

-Nuts

-Anything with Caffeine
